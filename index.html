EJISUMAN SENIOR HIGH SCHOOL

ABOUT

Ejisuman Senior High School, originally known as Ejisuman Secondary Technical School, was established on 13th September, 1970 by the Ejisu Youth Association to offer education at low cost to the children of Ejisu and surrounding towns and villages. It became a Public Assisted Secondary School in September, 1973 and was renamed Ejisuman Secondary School with the late Mr. J. E. Amoah as the first headmaster of the school. The school was moved from its old site to its present location at Ejisu Bafoso with a land size of 120 acres donated by Nana Dikopim III, the then Ejisumanhene of blessed memory. Later in 1989, the Government of Ghana added a 13.4 acre land which had seven bungalows on it and together with an undeveloped plot of land from the late Asare Konadu.Ejisuman Senior High School Vision To create an institution of excellence by providing an enabling environment to facilitate effective teaching and learning through upgrading pedagogical competencies of tutors and support from non-teaching staffs and community participation.
